Meet AlpaTrade — your personal trading floor → https://alpatrade.chat (clone it / fork it to run your own — sharing is caring)

A few things it can do that a normal trading dashboard can't 👇

🔑 Bring your own keys — connect your own Alpaca keys (Paper to practice, Production when you're ready) and off you go. Fernet-encrypted at rest, never leave your account. We never hold your funds — you trade through your own broker.
🎙️ Talk to it, don't just type — tap the mic for a real spoken conversation. Voice streams live audio + transcripts back through x.ai's realtime agent, right in the same window (the API key stays server-side).
🗺️ It answers with charts — ask "show me a market map" and it streams a finviz-style S&P treemap inline, coloured by return and sized by liquidity. Or a candlestick. Or a normalised multi-ticker compare.
📈 A full trading floor, not a chatbot — backtesting, paper trading, live positions & P&L, reconciliation, analyst ratings — all behind one calm chat.
📰 Real news, not hallucinated — headlines pulled live via Tavily search, grounded to the ticker you asked about.
⚙️ Provider-agnostic — pick your model / data / search provider from Settings (Grok / OpenAI / Claude — one dropdown), stored per user.
🔒 Bot-proof — a light sign-in protects the token budget without a paywall.

The stack (all open source, self-hosted):
• FastHTML — server-rendered, SSE streaming, no JS framework
• LangGraph agent, provider-agnostic (Grok / OpenAI / Claude — one env var)
• PostgreSQL + Docker on Coolify, auto-deploy on git push
• Eval suite (deepeval + LLM-as-judge) in the repo

⭐ Code: https://github.com/predictivelabsai/alpatrade
